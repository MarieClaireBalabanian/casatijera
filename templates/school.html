  • OAXACA EDUCATIONAL FOUNDATION

Oaxaca is rich with culture. Yet, at the same time, it is one of Mexico’ s poorest states. The regions illiteracy rate is a staggering 22.8%. That’ s where the Oaxaca Educational Foundation can make a difference.

We are establishing a school in partnership with the community of Vigia, in the municipality of San Pedro Pochutla, Oaxaca. Many of the children here endure difficult schoolroom conditions like extremely hot temperatures, primitive sanitation, parasitic infestation, unhygienic surroundings, and chronic poverty. Other schools lack teachers and appropriate curricula. We want to help Vigia build a stronger community through better schools.

The school in Vigia will welcome children from 3 to 5 years old. The preschool years are a time of critical brain development, and during that period parents should be especially involved in their child’ s education. We hope that the children in our classes will become lifelong learners, excelling both academically and ultimately outside of the classroom. Our preschool in Oaxaca will help develop the thoughts, feelings, and skills of Mexico’ s next generation.

Schools must focus on developing the whole child, and the school environment must build upon the foundations that children bring from their homes and communities, respecting their unique circumstances. The physical, social, aesthetic, spiritual, and cognitive needs of each child are inseparable from one another and are equally important. With that in mind, the Oaxaca Educational Foundation endeavors to provide the children of Vigia with an environment that enables them to become creative, competent, and responsible individuals.
